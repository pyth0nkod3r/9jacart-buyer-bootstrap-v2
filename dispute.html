Dispute Policy

Filing a Dispute

If you have a problem with an order or a seller, you can file a dispute within 14 days of delivery through your order details page.

Dispute Resolution Process

1. Contact the seller first through the order page to try to resolve the issue directly.
2. If unresolved within 48 hours, escalate the dispute to BuyerHub support.
3. Our team will review evidence from both parties and make a fair decision within 7 business days.

Evidence Required

Provide photos of the item received, communication records with the seller, and any other relevant documentation to support your claim.

Resolution Outcomes

Possible outcomes include full refund, partial refund, replacement, or dismissal of the dispute. Both parties will be notified of the final decision.
