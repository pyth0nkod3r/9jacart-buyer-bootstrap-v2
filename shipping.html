Shipping & Return Policy

Shipping Rates

Free shipping on orders above ₦50,000. Standard shipping fee of ₦2,500 applies to orders below this threshold.

Delivery Times

Standard delivery: 2-5 business days depending on location. Express delivery options are available at checkout for an additional fee.

Order Tracking

Track your order status in real-time through your account under "My Orders" → "Track Order".

Return Policy

Most products can be returned within 30 days of delivery in their original condition. Contact support to initiate a return.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, contact us within 48 hours of delivery for a full refund or replacement at no additional cost.

Packaging

All returned items must be in their original packaging. Products without original packaging may be subject to a restocking fee.
